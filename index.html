FAQ

Questions, answered.

What if I don't like the restaurant?
Rate it and tell us why. Our picks improve with every piece of feedback. If one truly misses the mark, we'll make it right.
How much will the actual dinner cost?
It varies. We pick from a wide range — sometimes a $35/person neighborhood spot, sometimes a $120 tasting menu. You'll always know the approximate price tier beforehand so there are no bill-shock surprises.
What about dietary restrictions?
Tell us once. We vet every menu and communicate with the restaurant before you arrive. Allergies, vegetarian, vegan, kosher, halal — all covered.

Built for couples who want to keep date night exciting. We're exploring options for friend groups down the line.
